Welcome to HoYoverse's open-world action RPG where you embark on an epic journey across the vast world of Teyvat. As a Traveler from another world, you awaken in this land filled with diverse cultures, dangerous enemies, and elemental magic. Your twin has been taken by an unknown god, and now you must explore the seven nations, each governed by a different element and Archon, to find answers and reunite with your sibling.

Along your journey, you'll meet unique characters with their own stories and abilities, face challenging enemies from common slimes to powerful weekly bosses, and uncover the ancient mysteries that lie beneath Teyvat's surface. Command the seven elements—Anemo, Geo, Electro, Dendro, Hydro, Pyro, and Cryo—to overcome obstacles, solve puzzles, and defeat enemies in dynamic combat.

Elemental Combat System

Master the seven elements and their reactions to gain tactical advantages. Combine Hydro and Cryo to freeze enemies, Pyro and Electro to trigger Overloaded explosions, or Dendro and Hydro to create Bloom seeds. Switch between four characters in your party to create powerful elemental combos and unleash devastating Elemental Bursts.

Exploration

Traverse diverse landscapes from mountains and forests to deserts and oceans. Climb any surface, glide across canyons, swim through rivers, and discover hidden treasures and challenges throughout the world. Unlock Teleport Waypoints and Statues of The Seven to fast-travel and restore your abilities.

Character Development

Build a team from over 50 unique characters, each with their own elemental abilities, weapons, and playstyles. Level up characters, weapons, and artifacts to increase power. Ascend characters to unlock their full potential and talents that enhance their combat capabilities.

Boss Battles

Test your skills against powerful enemies including Hypostases, Regisvines, and Weekly Bosses. Each boss has unique attack patterns and vulnerabilities to specific elements. Defeat them to collect materials needed for character ascension and talent upgrades.

Gacha System

Use Primogems and Wishes to obtain new characters and weapons through the gacha system. Characters range from 4-star to 5-star rarity, each with unique abilities that can complement your team composition. Limited-time event banners feature exclusive characters and weapons.

Daily Activities

Complete Daily Commissions to earn Adventure Experience and Primogems. Use Original Resin to claim rewards from Domains, Ley Line Outcrops, and World Bosses. Participate in events that offer unique challenges, stories, and rewards.

Running & Sprinting

Basic movement that consumes stamina. Press the sprint button to move faster across the terrain. Sprinting depletes your stamina bar quickly but allows you to cover more ground in less time. Increase your stamina capacity by offering Anemoculus and Geoculus to Statues of The Seven.

Climbing

Scale mountains, cliffs, and structures by approaching them and moving forward. Climbing consumes stamina gradually, and you'll fall if your stamina runs out. Plan your climbing routes carefully and use stamina food to climb higher peaks.

Gliding

Deploy your wind glider to soar through the air. Jump from high places and press the glide button to spread your wings. Gliding consumes stamina slowly, allowing you to cover large distances and reach otherwise inaccessible areas. Upgrade your glider through special quests.

Swimming

Traverse bodies of water by swimming on the surface or diving underwater. Regular swimming and fast swimming both consume stamina. Beware of running out of stamina while far from shore! Collect and use stamina food before long swims.

Teleporting

Fast travel between unlocked Teleport Waypoints, Domains, and Statues of The Seven. Open your map and select a discovered waypoint to instantly transport your character to that location. Unlock new teleport points by exploring the world and interacting with these special markers.

Normal Attacks

Basic melee or ranged attacks performed by characters, varying based on their equipped weapon (Sword, Claymore, Polearm, Bow, or Catalyst). These attacks form a combo sequence with multiple hits.

Charged Attacks

A stronger attack that consumes stamina. Each weapon type has a unique charged attack: Swords & Polearms: A powerful thrust or slash. Claymores: A spinning attack followed by a strong final hit. Bows: A charged shot that can infuse an element at higher charge levels. Catalysts: An enhanced magical projectile infused with the user’s element.

Plunging Attacks

Performed by attacking while mid-air. Upon landing, the attack deals AoE (Area of Effect) damage, with higher damage if the fall is from a great height. Some characters have special plunge attack variations.

Dodging

Sprinting or dashing provides invincibility frames (i-frames), allowing characters to avoid enemy attacks if timed correctly. Certain characters, like Mona and Ayaka, have unique sprint abilities that also apply elemental effects.

Shielding

Some characters (e.g., Zhongli, Noelle, Diona) can generate shields that absorb damage. Shields have different strengths and can provide elemental resistance based on the user’s abilities. Stronger shields (like Zhongli’s) can also reduce enemy resistance.

Healing

Characters with healing abilities (e.g., Barbara, Bennett, Kokomi, Jean) can restore HP for themselves or the party. Healing can also be done using food items, some of which have cooldowns or limitations in co-op mode. Certain weapons and artifacts enhance healing effectiveness.

Mondstadt

One of the seven regions of Teyvat, and the first in which the Traveler starts to look for their lost sibling. It is the nation that worships Lord Barbatos, theAnemo Archon and God of Freedom.

A city of freedom that lies in the northeast of Teyvat. From amongst mountains and wide-open plains, carefree breezes carry the scent of dandelions a gift from the Anemo God, Barbatos across Cider Lake to Mondstadt, which sits on an island in the middle of the lake.

Storm Terror's Lair

is a subarea located in Brightcrown Mountains, Mondstadt.It was once the capital of Old Mondstadt, ruled by the God of Storms, Decarabian. After he was slain in an uprising, Barbatos took his place and reshaped the land. The people then moved the capital to an island in the middle of Cider Lake, where the City of Mondstadt remains to this day.













Barbatos

is the God of Freedom and The Seven's Anemo Archon, presiding over Mondstadt.

Along with Morax, he is one of The Seven's two remaining original members and the second-oldest of The Seven.




Liyue (Chinese: 璃月 Líyuè)

One of the seven regions of Teyvat. It is the nation that worships Rex Lapis, the Geo Archon and God of Contracts.

A bountiful harbor that lies in the east of Teyvat. Mountains stand tall and proud alongside the stone forest, that, together with the open plains and lively rivers, make up Liyue's bountiful landscape, which shows its unique beauty through each of the four seasons. Just how many gifts from the Geo God lie in wait amongst the rocks of Liyue's mountains?

The Jade Chamber

Is a point of interest and an instance located in Mt. Tianheng, Sea of Clouds, Liyue. It is an airborne structure above the skies of Liyue from which the Qixing monitor all the happenings in the region.

Rex Lapis

also known by his Goetic name Morax, is the God of Contracts and a member of The Seven. He was the Geo Archon who presided over Liyue








Inazuma (Japanese: 稲妻 Inazuma)

one of the seven regions of Teyvat. It is an archipelagic region that worships Raiden Shogun, the Electro Archon and God of Eternitywho is also the leader of its governing body, the Inazuma Shogunate.

Inazuma is located approximately 4 km southeast from Liyue Harbor and closely neighbors the Dark Sea.

Narukami Shrine

Is a point of interest and an instance located in Mt. Tianheng, Sea of Clouds, Liyue. It is an airborne structure above the skies of Liyue from which the Qixing monitor all the happenings in the region.

Raiden Ei (Japanese: 雷電影 Raiden Ei),

also known by her Goetic name Beelzebul and as the Raiden Shogun, is the God of Eternity and the current Electro Archon presiding over Inazuma.












Sumeru

One of the seven regions of Teyvat. It is the nation that worships Lesser Lord Kusanali, the Dendro Archon and God of Wisdom. It is renowned as Teyvat's center of learning for housing the Sumeru Akademiya.

The city of scholars located in the west-central part of Teyvat. A fantastical nation of both lush rainforest and barren desert, where countless fruits of wisdom grow and are buried. Whether Travelers travel from afar through the forest to reach the academy city or delve deep into the desert to discover the historical ruins of the red desert, a wealth of valuable knowledge awaits them here.

Zubayr Theater

Lesser Lord Kusanali

also known by her Goetic name Buer and as Nahida, is the God of Wisdom and the current Dendro Archon presiding over Sumeru.












Fontaine

One of the seven regions of Teyvat. It is the nation that worshipped Focalors, the Hydro Archon and God of Justice.

A terrestrial sea in the center of Teyvat. Following the direction of pure currents, crossing wilderness, the depths of the forests and vastness of the sea of sand, arriving at the origin of all the waters of the continent. At the top of the waterfall, in the depths of the capital atop the terrestrial sea... a story that has never been heard, a legend that has been forgotten, like a lost kingdom sunken beneath the waves, yearning for a bard to sing its drowned songs.

Focalors

was the God of Justice and the former Hydro Archon among The Seven Focalors, the Hydro Archon and God of Justice.

presiding over Fontaine as the successor of Egeria. Prior to her ascension, she was originally one of Egeria's Oceanid familiars. After her ascension, Focalors separated her "divinity" from her "body and spirit" to exist separately as Focalors, while the body and "humanity" became Furina.








Natlan

One of the seven regions of Teyvat. It is the nation that worships Mavuika, the Pyro Archon and God of War. The nation of oaths situated in the western regions of Teyvat. Guided by the flame, you pass through lengthy gorges and valleys to reach the land where the torches blaze, where all fires converge.

Upon the blazing fields where Saurians roam, witness the end of the ancient war alongside the valiant warriors who resist the darkness.

Saurians

are Natlan creatures that provide unique exploration abilities through the Indewlling mechanic in Genshin Impact 5.0. See all of the playable Saurian types and abilities, their locations, how to rename your Saurian, and everything we know in this guide!

Mavuika

also known by her Goetic name Haborym and as Acient name Kiongozi, is the current God of War and Pyro Archon presiding over Natlan.
